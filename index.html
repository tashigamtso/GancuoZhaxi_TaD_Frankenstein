Frankenstein

by Mary Wollstonecraft Shelley

Welcome to the mini digital edition of Mary Shelley's Frankenstein. Made by Gancuo Zhaxi as a project for Text as Data taught at the University of Antwerp.

Mary Wollstonecraft Shelley was an English novelist known for writing Frankenstein, which is considered one of the earliest works of science fiction. She and her husband William Godwin, together with their daughter Mary Wollstonecraft Godwin Shelley and her husband, Percy Bysshe Shelley, have been justly called England’s first family of writers. The digitized verision of their widely dispersed hanwritten legacy can be reached in the following site The Shelly Godwin Archive

This little project of digital edition focuses on chapter 7A of Bodleian MS. Abinger’s c.56 manuscript, and is encoded according to BDMP Encoding Manual. All 10 pages of the relative chapter are included as follow:
